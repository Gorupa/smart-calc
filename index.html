Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 2026

This application was built with a strict offline-first, privacy-by-design architecture. Here is how we handle your data:

  • Zero Tracking: We do not use Google Analytics, Firebase, or any other telemetry to track your usage.
  • Zero Data Collection: All calculations are performed locally on your device. No financial data is ever sent to an external server.
  • Offline Capability: Once loaded, this app functions entirely without an internet connection.
  • No Ads: There are no ad networks embedded in this application.
